Story about Watch Store

At the beginning, we realized that the watch market in Vietnam still had many limitations in terms of models, brands, high prices and most importantly, the product origin was not guaranteed.

In addition, showrooms and watch shops at that time only operated in the traditional way with available old models, depending heavily on the geography of each province. Customers do not have many choices and benefits are not guaranteed. Watch store was established with the purpose of redefining the aging market, trying to bring a lot of choices, good quality products, reputable brands with comprehensive warranty policy, providing absolute peace of mind. when shopping for customers. And above all, bringing world-class watch masterpieces closer to Vietnamese consumers, helping customers own genuine products at a good price. Currently, Watch store is proud to become one of the leading prestigious and professional genuine watch distribution systems in Vietnam with more than 40 world-renowned brands.

Service Motto:

Through a team of dedicated and objective consultants, helping customers feel secure about owning genuine quality products with guaranteed quality, dedicated after-sales service with good discounts. Continuing to improve, becoming a genuine watch distribution system with a diverse product portfolio and brands. As a result, LikeWatch's customers can easily compare and choose products that are suitable for their personality, purpose, and style.


Commitment in business

Watch store is committed to selling only genuine products from world-class, quality brands, imported from genuine distribution centers from the US and Europe.

- 100% new product, in original box, catalog, warranty card, user manual and all accessories provided by the company for that product line.
- Commitment to global warranty policy as prescribed by each brand.
- Always deploying many incentive programs so that customers can easily own their favorite products and brands at the best discounted prices.
- Improve service quality by improving the knowledge and skills of the consultant team, putting the customer's shopping experience and after-sales policy first.
